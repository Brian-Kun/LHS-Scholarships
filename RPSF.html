Red Pine Scholarship


Description: The Red Pine Scholarship welcomes its second class of Scholars from across Massachusetts, who have demonstrated academic excellence and are attending competitive colleges as freshmen in the fall of 2015. This program is designed to support students through tuition assistance, mentoring, and other flexible financial supports. The Red Pine Scholarship program is dedicated to making college affordable for Massachusetts families and helping students to reach their full potential. The program is funded by an anonymous donor and administered by The Philanthropic Initiative (TPI).


Deadline: Available soon


Requirements:

  • Must have an adjusted gross family income of $80,000 or less and have substantial unmet need (at least $5000)
  • Applicants must be involved in a balance of community, school, and work activities, and demonstrate an ability to articulate goals and future plans.
  • Applicants also must be permanent residents of Massachusetts and US citizens or eligible non-citizens who are eligible to receive financial need (have completed the FAFSA).
