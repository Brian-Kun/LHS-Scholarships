Rise Scholarship Fund


Description: The RiSE Scholarship Foundation, Inc. is a non-profit resource and scholarship opportunity for high school students who learn differently. Created in 2010, RiSE has awarded over 70 scholarship awards to deserving students throughout the United States. The awarded high school seniors have much to share through their journey.


Deadline: Available soon


Requirements:

  • Must have a grade point average (GPA) of 2.5 or higher on a 4 point scale
  • Must be a U.S. citizen
  • Must include a photograph with student’s name written on the back of it
  • Must have a documented learning disability, and or an ASD diagnosis
  • Must provide a letter of reference from a teacher, high school counselor, or principal of present high school
