DCF Adopted Child Tuition Waiver and Fee Assistance Program


Description: The DCF Adopted Child Tuition Waiver and Fee Assistance Program is designed to lessen the financial burden on adopting parents in the Commonwealth. This waiver extends eligibility to all children and young adults, age twenty-four or under, adopted through DCF by state employees or eligible Massachusetts residents, regardless of the date of adoption. The tuition and fee waiver encompasses 100% of tuition and fees for state-supported courses, subject to budgetary appropriation, at all of the Massachusetts public institutions of higher education, excluding graduate courses and courses in the MD program at the University of Massachusetts Medical Center.


Deadline: Not available


Requirements:

  • Must complete FAFSA
  • Adopting parent(s) lived in MA at time of adoption
  • Student adopted through DCF
