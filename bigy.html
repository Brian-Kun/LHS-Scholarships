Description: Big Y Foods Inc. is one of the largest, locally owned supermarket chains in Southern New England. Big Y offers an annual scholarship program. The program includes a series of scholarships. All Big Y scholarships recognize academic merit and achievement. They are awarded to the most academically outstanding students in the Big Y market area. There are awards for both traditional and nontraditional students at the undergraduate and graduate levels.


Deadline: Not available


Requirements:

  • Transcript of grades
  • College Entrance Exams Scores
  • At least two letters of recommendation, including at least one from a teacher
  • Personal resume/fact sheet Activities
