NAAAP Boston


Description: Started over 20 years ago, the purpose of the Future Leaders Scholarship fund has been to recognize and celebrate outstanding high school seniors who have demonstrated leadership qualities, community service, and high academic achievement in the Boston and surrounding areas. The Future Leader Award is a scholarship for aspiring Asian American leaders. The Scholarship Committee prioritizes candidates with demonstrated financial need and evaluates these candidates on academic achievement, extracurricular activities, community service, and leadership related experiences.


Deadline: Not available


Requirements:

  • Involvement in community, school
  • Financial need - academic achievement and leadership skill
  • 500 word (2) essays; (2) letters of recommendation, SAR, SAT/ACT test scores, transcript, self addressed stamped post card
  • Must be of Asian ethnicity
