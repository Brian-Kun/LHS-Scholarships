John D. O'Bryant Scholarship


Description: Each year, RMSC is able to offer scholarships to high school seniors that have been accepted into an accredited college, university or higher learning institution. Awards are issued based on financial need or academic merit. Scholarship Funds have been made available through our partnership with the Boston Foundation and the generous donation of the Ruby Emerson Fund and Harold T. Peabody Memorial Fund.


Deadline: Not available


Requirements:

  • 500 word; 3 recs
  • Preference given to residents of Roxbury, Dorchester, Mattapan
  • Must have a 3.0 GPA
