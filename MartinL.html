Martin Luther King Memorial


Description: This CTA scholarship is given in memory of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. to aid members of ethnic minorities in pursuing a college degree, credential or certification for a teaching-related career in public education. Applicant must be an "active" CTA member, Student CTA member or dependent of a CTA member.
